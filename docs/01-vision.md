# OliveB2B Vision

## Purpose
OliveB2B is a B2B marketplace that connects suppliers and professionals with buyers. The platform is focused on discovery and direct interaction, without intermediary payments or commissions.

## Roles
- Supplier (Enterprise)
- Supplier (Private)
- Professional
- Buyer
- Admin

## Marketplace Rules
- No payments and no commissions.
- Buyers can create RFQs.
- Suppliers and professionals interact directly with buyers.
- No admin intervention in transactions or communications.

## Access Gating
- Guests can only see brief summaries.
- Full details, supplier identity, and contact information require login.
- Only registered users can create RFQs and Offers.
